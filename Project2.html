BetterWorldNow.


Citizens are in dire need of a more interactive and fun way to contribute towards the betterment of the environment, as the current methods make the problem seem farfetched and difficult to solve.

Our motivation is derived from the stimuli that many people all over the world want to contribute towards the amelioration of the environment but either find it hard to know where to start or assume that the problem is unachievable from their end. On the other end of the spectrum, many people have not thought about environmental welfare as they are not prompted properly to do so. The lack of a platform which would educate and indulge people in willfully participating in this activity for the greater good is what fueled us further to go for this project.

The BETTER WORLD NOW initiative aims to unite like-minded people for the noble cause of doing our small yet utmost duty towards helping the environment and, in turn, saving humankind in the long run. We want to provide a one-stop solution platform to educate people about environmental welfare and give more significant insights into how their small part has positively impacted the earth on a grander scale. It would give users more significant incentives like monetary aid and self-development boost to further help the cause. The initiative will provide users with a friendly rating system for becoming a responsible and good samaritans.
