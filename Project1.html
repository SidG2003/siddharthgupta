Hyper personalised 3D
Lamp Model.


In today’s time, most homes have become closed and there’s a dissociation between the outside and the inside. I am more fond of older homes, as they never make you feel trapped. The lamp that I’ve made takes a step towards bridging this gap between the insides of our homes and the natural world outside along with adding to the aesthetics of the interior. Thus it fulfils this requirement.

The lamp takes inspiration from the structure of trees, but also does not fully simulate it in order to make it look like a designed product and not just a tree model. To fulfil the same purpose of keeping it surreal, the colours used are not conventional tree colours, neither are the leaves.
